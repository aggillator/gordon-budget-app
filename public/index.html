"Find internal transfers" catches the same real expense showing up twice because money moved through two connected accounts (e.g. PayPal funded by a card). "Find refunded purchases" catches a charge and its own refund on the SAME account, so a canceled purchase doesn't still count as spending. Both exclude the matched transactions from your budget - run either after syncing new activity.

Upload a CSV export from your bank or credit card. Pick which account it belongs to, map the columns below, and the transactions will be added the same way a Plaid sync would - safe to re-upload the same file, duplicates are merged automatically. Also automatically skips any row that's already covered by a real Plaid sync, so this and your bank connection can't create two copies of the same transaction.

Upload a CSV export of your Amazon order history. Item titles get matched to your existing Amazon transactions by amount + date and applied as the transaction name - your bank data isn't changed, just how it displays. Never overwrites a transaction you've already renamed.
